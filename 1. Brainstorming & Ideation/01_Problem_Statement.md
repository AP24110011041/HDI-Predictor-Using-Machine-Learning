# Problem Statement

## Background

The Human Development Index (HDI) is an important indicator used to measure the overall development of a country. It considers factors such as life expectancy, education, and income to evaluate the quality of life and human development.

## Problem

Traditional methods of analyzing and estimating HDI require collecting and processing large amounts of socio-economic data. This process can be time-consuming and requires technical knowledge for analysis and interpretation.

Researchers, students, and policy analysts often need a quick and reliable way to estimate HDI based on available indicators.

## Proposed Solution

This project develops a Machine Learning-based prediction system that estimates the Human Development Index using selected socio-economic features. The trained model is integrated into a Flask web application, allowing users to enter input values and receive an instant HDI prediction through a simple interface.
